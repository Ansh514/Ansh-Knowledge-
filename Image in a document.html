    4. What is wrap text around images ?
    Ans :- When you place image in a document , you may need to position text around the image in multiple ways. This is known as wrapping text around image.
    5. What are possible ways of wrapping text around image in writer ?
    Ans :- The possible ways of wrapping text around image is writer or as follow :
  • None : with this option the text is placed above and below the image but not around it .
  • Before : the text flows before the image. after the text flows after the image .
  • Parallel : the text lose before and after the image.
  • Wrap Through : superimposes the image on the text. That is , the image is above the text. The image will be transparent.
  • Optimal : the text flows around the image. Moving the image around the text causes the text to be rearranged to fill the space.

    7. What is cropping ? How is it useful ?
    Ans :- Cropping means removing unwanted area from an image starting from its boundary.
    It is useful as it allows the user to remove the unwanted part of the image .
    Note that :- cropping removes a continuous section i.e., you cannot remove a random part of the image.
