1. What is style.
Ans :- Style in a word processor, is a named collection of various formatting features / informations that defines the look and behaviour of document components associated with this style .
1. What are character styles ? How are these different from paragraph styles.
Ans :- Characteristics affect a block of text inside a paragraph . It impact only the seglected characters of a paragraph. Character styles are applied to selected group of characters in a paragraph where as paragraph styles are apply to whole paragraph.
Characters style are mainly used when you want to change the appearance or attribute of parts of a paragraph without affecting the other parts.
3. How is Fill Format useful for applying styles?
Ans :-This method is useful when we need to format many scattered paragraphs, words or other items with the same style, and it may be easier to use than making multiple selection first and then applying a style to all of them .

    4.What are different categories of styles in writer ?
    Ans :-The different categories of the styles in writer are :

  • Character style :- used to format a single character, or an entire word.
  • Paragraph style :- used to format paragraphs, including the form type and size .
  • Frame style :- used to format text and graphic frames.
  • Page style :- used to organised the structure of the document .
  • List style :- used to format number of bulleted lists .
